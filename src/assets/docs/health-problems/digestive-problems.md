# Digestive problems

## Symptoms
- Abdominal pain;
- Bleeding;
- Bloating;
- Constipation;
- Diarrhea;
- Gas;
- Heartburn;
- Indigestion;
- Incontinence;
- Nausea;
- Swallowing problems;
- Vomiting;
- Weight gain or loss.

## Diseases

### Esophagus disease
- Achalasia;
- Esophagus narrowing.

### Gallbladder disease
- Cholecystitis;
- Gallstones.

### Intestinal disease
- Celiac disease;
- Crohn's disease;
- Diverticulitis;
- Infections;
- Intestinal ischemia;
- Malabsorption;
- Polyps;
- Short bowel syndrome;
- Ulcerative colitis.

### Liver disease
- Cholangitis;
- Cirrhosis;
- Hepatitis.

### Pancreas disease
- Cancer;
- Pancreatitis;
- Pancreatic pseudocyst.

### Rectal disease
- Anal fissure;
- Hemorrhoids;
- Proctitis;
- Rectal prolapse.

### Stomach disease
- Gastritis;
- Gastroparesis;
- Crohn's disease;
- Ulcers.
